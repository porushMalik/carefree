WEIGHT GAIN

Why?

It is beneficial to gain healthy weight. Maintaining a healthy weight can lower the risk of heart disease, stroke, diabetes, high blood pressure, and many different cancers.

How to check if you are underweight

If your BMI<19 then you are underweight

BMI formula

BMI = Body weight(kg)/Height (m2)

Dietery Changes

Increase intake of protein, carbohydrates, and healthy fats.

Nuts: almonds, walnuts, macadamia nuts, and peanuts
Dried fruit: raisins, dates, prunes, and others
High fat dairy: whole milk, yogurt, and cream
Grains: oats, brown rice
Tubers: potatoes, sweet potatoes, and yams
Other energy-dense foods: dark chocolate, peanut butter, coconut milk

Change your lifestyle

Avoid drinking water before meals

Eat more often

Get quality sleep

if you smoke, consider quitting
